🌿 Top Eco Event Ideas for Youth Eco Lead

1. 🌳 "Adopt-a-Tree" Campaign

  • Youth pledge to plant and care for a tree at school/home/community.
  • Give each tree a name and track growth.
  • Great for visibility and donor reporting.

2. 🧹 Green Clean-Up Drive

  • Host monthly cleanups in towns, riversides, and markets.
  • Partner with local government or NEMA.
  • Add youth music, banners, and giveaways to make it fun!

3. ♻️ Trash to Treasure Workshop

  • Train youth to turn plastic, cans, and paper into usable art/products.
  • Invite local artisans or crafters to co-lead.
  • Sell or donate outputs.

4. 📚 School Eco-Education Days

  • Visit schools with fun eco quizzes, games, and talks.
  • Include tree planting, competitions, and storytelling.
  • Reward best “Green Ambassadors.”

5. 📅 Eco Challenge Week

  • Day 1: Carry your water bottle
  • Day 2: No plastic
  • Day 3: Plant something
  • Day 4: Walk or cycle only
  • Day 5: Share an eco tip on social media

6. 🎥 "Youths Greening the World" Film Night

  • Outdoor or school-based screening of documentaries or local eco films.
  • Use it to raise awareness or raise donations.

7. 🧪 Green Innovation Pitch Day

  • Let youth pitch eco-solutions (water harvesting, energy, waste).
  • Invite donors and judges.
  • Offer starter seed funding to winners.

8. 📦 Eco Donation Drive

  • Collect used clothes, shoes, books for reuse/donation.
  • Combine with recycling efforts.

9. 🏞️ Nature Hike & Talk

  • Take youths on a forest walk or nature trail.
  • Invite an ecologist to teach tree/plant identification.
  • End with a reflection circle or picnic.

10. 🛠️ Build an Eco Garden

  • Set up a vegetable garden at a school or community center.
  • Teach composting, drip irrigation, mulching.
  • Involve parents and local farmers.
